研究经历 | Research Experience

🔥 带电加速黑洞热力学研究 (2023-2025)

指导老师：Robert Mann 教授

重新考虑了描述带电（缓慢）加速AdS黑洞的各种C-度规时空，并重新审视了它们的热力学性质。发现了一种度规的参数化方法，可以消除在以前的研究中对获得一致热力学至关重要的非平凡"归一化"。

🌟 智能洞：具有正确温度和熵的类比黑洞 (2022-2024)

指导老师：Niayesh Afshordi 教授

在类比引力研究中，我们的目标是在受控的实验室环境中复制黑洞现象。我们计算了这个系统的熵，发现如果形成类比视界，它具有与黑洞Bekenstein-Hawking熵相同的形式。

🕳️ 爱因斯坦立方引力中真空虫洞的存在 (2023-2024)

指导老师：Robert Mann 教授

提出了爱因斯坦立方引力场方程的虫洞解，这是广义相对论的现象学竞争者，包括曲率的立方项，没有物质，外来物质或其他物质。这些纯引力虫洞几何是渐近AdS的，但在无穷远处包含几何缺陷。

⚡ 黑洞四重点附近相变的动力学行为 (2022-2023)

指导老师：Robert Mann 教授

将视界半径作为热涨落中的序参量，自由能景观模型揭示了黑洞相变的动力学行为。我们首次研究了最近发现的黑洞多临界性的动力学。

📐 复杂度、标度和相变 (2021)

指导老师：Andrew Frey 教授 (Mitacs Globalink项目)

研究了在圆上紧化且带有Wilson线的CFT的全息复杂度，该理论对偶于AdS₄和AdS₅中的磁化孤子。这些理论作为Wilson线的函数具有禁闭-去禁闭相变，形成复杂度作为这种相变的序参量。

Research Experience | 研究经历

🔥 Thermodynamics of Charged and Accelerating Black Holes (2023-2025)

Advisor: Prof. Robert Mann

Reconsidered various C-metric spacetimes describing charged and (slowly) accelerating AdS black holes and revisited their thermodynamic properties. Found a parametrization of the metric where we can eliminate the non-trivial 'normalization' crucial for obtaining consistent thermodynamics.

🌟 Smart Holes: Analogue Black Holes with the Right Temperature and Entropy (2022-2024)

Advisor: Prof. Niayesh Afshordi

In analogue gravity studies, the goal is to replicate black hole phenomena within controlled laboratory settings. We computed the entropy of this system and found it has the same form as black hole Bekenstein-Hawking entropy if an analogue horizon forms.

🕳️ Existence of Vacuum Wormholes in Einsteinian Cubic Gravity (2023-2024)

Advisor: Prof. Robert Mann

Presented a wormhole solution to the field equations of Einsteinian Cubic Gravity, a phenomenological competitor to general relativity that includes terms cubic in the curvature with no matter, exotic or otherwise. These purely gravitational wormhole geometries are asymptotically AdS.

⚡ Dynamic Behaviours of Black Hole Phase Transitions Near Quadruple Points (2022-2023)

Advisor: Prof. Robert Mann

Treating the horizon radius as an order parameter in thermal fluctuation, the free energy landscape model sheds light on dynamic behaviour of black hole phase transitions. We carried out the first investigation of dynamics of recently discovered multicriticality in black holes.

📐 Complexity, Scaling, and a Phase Transition (2021)

Advisor: Prof. Andrew Frey (Mitacs Globalink Project)

Investigated holographic complexity of CFTs compactified on a circle with a Wilson line, dual to magnetized solitons in AdS₄ and AdS₅. These theories have a confinement-deconfinement phase transition as a function of the Wilson line, and complexity of formation acts as an order parameter.
