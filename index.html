Advisor: Prof. Robert Mann

Reconsidered various C-metric spacetimes describing charged and (slowly) accelerating AdS black holes and revisited their thermodynamic properties. Found a parametrization of the metric where we can eliminate the non-trivial 'normalization' crucial for obtaining consistent thermodynamics.

ğŸŒŸ Smart Holes: Analogue Black Holes with the Right Temperature and Entropy (2022-2024)

Advisor: Prof. Niayesh Afshordi

In analogue gravity studies, the goal is to replicate black hole phenomena within controlled laboratory settings. We computed the entropy of this system and found it has the same form as black hole Bekenstein-Hawking entropy if an analogue horizon forms.
