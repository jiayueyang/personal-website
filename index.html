关于我 | About Me

我是杨佳悦，目前在加拿大滑铁卢大学攻读应用数学博士学位，同时也是周界理论物理研究所(Perimeter Institute)和量子计算研究所(Institute for Quantum Computing)的研究生。我的研究领域主要集中在量子信息与量子引力的交叉领域。

🔬 研究兴趣

  • 量子信息与量子计算 (QIQC)
  • 量子引力中的量子信息 (QIQG)
  • 相对论性量子信息 (RQI)

🌟 科研亮点

我的研究涵盖了从黑洞热力学到全息复杂度，从虫洞解到类比黑洞等多个前沿领域。特别是在黑洞相变动力学和量子引力中的复杂度理论方面取得了重要进展。

About Me | 关于我

I am Jiayue Yang, currently pursuing a Ph.D. student in applied mathematics at the University of Waterloo, Canada, while also serving as an Associate Graduate Student at the Perimeter Institute (PI) and Institute for Quantum Computing (IQC). My research focuses on the intersection of quantum information and quantum gravity.

🌟 Research Highlights

My research spans from black hole thermodynamics to holographic complexity, from wormhole solutions to analogue black holes. I have made significant contributions to the dynamics of black hole phase transitions and complexity theory in quantum gravity.

研究 | Research

[1] Complexity, Scaling, and a Phase Transition

Yang, J., Frey, A.R. Journal of High Energy Physics 2023, 29 (2023)

研究了圆上紧化CFT的全息复杂度，发现复杂度在相变中起到序参量的作用。

[2] Dynamic Behaviours of Black Hole Phase Transitions Near Quadruple Points

Yang, J., Mann, R.B. Journal of High Energy Physics 2023, 28 (2023)

首次研究了黑洞四重点附近的动力学行为，发现了丰富的相变现象。

[3] Gravitational Wormholes

Lu, M., Yang, J., Mann, R. B. Universe, 10(6), 257 (2024)

综述了引力虫洞的理论研究，特别是在高阶曲率理论中的新进展。

[4] Existence of Vacuum Wormholes in Einsteinian Cubic Gravity

Lu, M., Yang, J., Mann, R. B. Accepted by JHEP (2024)

在爱因斯坦立方引力理论中发现了不需要外来物质的真空虫洞解。

[5] Smart Holes: Analogue Black Holes with the Right Temperature and Entropy

Yang, J., Afshordi, N., Torabian, M., Jafari, S. A., Baskaran, G. Submitted to JHEP (2024)

提出了新型类比黑洞"智能洞"，可以同时模拟黑洞的温度和熵。

[6] On Thermodynamics of Charged and Accelerating Black Holes

Hale, T., Kubizňák, D., Menšíková, J., Mann, R. B, Yang, J. Submitted to PRD (2025)

重新审视了带电加速黑洞的热力学性质，发现了新的理论问题。

Research | 研究

[1] Complexity, Scaling, and a Phase Transition

Yang, J., Frey, A.R. Journal of High Energy Physics 2023, 29 (2023)

Investigated holographic complexity of CFTs on a circle, finding complexity acts as an order parameter for phase transitions.

[2] Dynamic Behaviours of Black Hole Phase Transitions Near Quadruple Points

Yang, J., Mann, R.B. Journal of High Energy Physics 2023, 28 (2023)

First investigation of dynamics near black hole quadruple points, revealing rich phase transition phenomena.

[3] Gravitational Wormholes

Lu, M., Yang, J., Mann, R. B. Universe, 10(6), 257 (2024)

Comprehensive review of gravitational wormholes, especially new developments in higher-curvature theories.

[4] Existence of Vacuum Wormholes in Einsteinian Cubic Gravity

Lu, M., Yang, J., Mann, R. B. Accepted by JHEP (2024)

Discovered vacuum wormhole solutions in Einsteinian Cubic Gravity without exotic matter.

[5] Smart Holes: Analogue Black Holes with the Right Temperature and Entropy

Yang, J., Afshordi, N., Torabian, M., Jafari, S. A., Baskaran, G. Submitted to JHEP (2024)

Proposed novel analogue black holes "smart holes" that can simulate both temperature and entropy of real black holes.

[6] On Thermodynamics of Charged and Accelerating Black Holes

Hale, T., Kubizňák, D., Menšíková, J., Mann, R. B, Yang, J. Submitted to PRD (2025)

Reconsidered thermodynamics of charged accelerating black holes, uncovering new theoretical issues.
